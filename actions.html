Action recommendations

Enhance emotional support:

To enhance your emotional support for each other, you could introduce a 'safe word' system. This would act as an emotional SOS, alerting your partner to your distress and signaling a need for immediate empathy and understanding.

Encourage open dialogue:

To encourage more open dialogue on your wants and hopes for the relationship, try setting aside a specific time each week for a 'deep talk'. Use this time to discuss everything openly, from your smallest desires to your grandest dreams, without fear of judgment or interruption.

Continue deepening communication:

Given that your relationship is relatively new (less than a year old), remember to take things slow and gradually build your communication on deeper, more sensitive issues. Start with smaller concerns and work your way up as your comfort level increases.
